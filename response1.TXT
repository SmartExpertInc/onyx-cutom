custom_backend-1  |
custom_backend-1  | Cost-plus pricing is a straightforward approach where businesses calculate the total cost of producing a product and then add a markup percentage to determine the selling price. This method ensures that all costs are covered while providing a profit margin.

custom_backend-1  | - **Advantages**: Simple to calculate and ensures costs are covered.
