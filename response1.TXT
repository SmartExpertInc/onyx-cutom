custom_backend-1  |
custom_backend-1  | Cost-plus pricing is a straightforward method where businesses calculate the total cost of producing a product and then add a markup to determine the selling price.
custom_backend-1  |
custom_backend-1  | - **Advantages**: Simple to calculate and ensures all costs are covered.
custom_backend-1  | - **Disadvantages**: Ignores market demand and competitor pricing, which can lead to overpricing or underpricing.
custom_backend-1  | - **Best For**: Industries with stable costs and low competition.
